# What are Life Milestones?
**Life Milestones are selected important achivements in my life**. On average only one achivement per year is worth mentioning here, which means, from another perspective,  you are selecting roughly the top 80 events to summarize your whole life. The selection is very subjective so the milestones displayed below are also subject to change.
